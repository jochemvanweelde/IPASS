The led will blink on and off.

The led writes an 1 and a 0 for a couple of times.
int ms is the duraion between turning the led on and off.
int times is how many times the led is going to be turned on. Example: blink(pin, 100, 2); will blink 2 times with 100ms every on/off turn. Accpets every led_pin -> ws2812led.
